Blur - Smart Privacy Protection for Screen Sharing
Blur is a browser extension that automatically detects and obscures sensitive information during screen sharing sessions on Zoom, Google Meet, and other platforms. Protect confidential business data, customer information, and personal details from accidental exposure. Â
